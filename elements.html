Intrinsic Ratios

Intrinsic ratios are a useful trick for helping interactives, flash videos, and galleries to be fluidly responsive. First documented as "Uncle Dave's Ol' Padded Box. Ratios taken from the wikipedia Aspect Ratio (image) article.

.mt_intratio--tv (4:3)

.mt_intratio--imax (1.43:1)

.mt_intratio--photo (3:2)

.mt_intratio--golden (1.618:1)

.mt_intratio--broadcast (16:9)

.intratio--widescreen (1.85:1)
