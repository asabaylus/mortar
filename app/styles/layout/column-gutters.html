Column Gutters: The padding between columns is configurable and is available in the following classes: none, small, medium, and large. These can also be configured for each breakpoint.

 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 

Responsive Gutters: Use the breakpoint classes to additionally grow or shrink gutters at different breakpoints.
